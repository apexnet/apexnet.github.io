Apex Launcher

About

The Apex Launcher is a modified version of Technic from HackPhoenix.com built for ApexNet servers and uses the ApexNet authentication servers. Make sure to sign in with your ApexNet username. On most servers, you must enter a PIN every login. You get this username and PIN when you join ApexNet.
